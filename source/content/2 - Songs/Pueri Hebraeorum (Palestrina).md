# Pueri Hebraeorum (Palestrina)

## Lyrics
[CPDL - Latin with Translation](https://www.cpdl.org/wiki/index.php/Pueri_Hebraeorum,_portantes)  
```
Pueri Hebraeorum,                   The Hebrew children
portantes ramos olivarum,           bearing olive branches,
obviaverunt Domino,                 went forth to meet the Lord,
clamantes et dicentes:              crying out, and saying:
Hosanna in excelsis.                Hosanna in the highest.

Pueri Hebraeorum,                   The children of the Hebrews
vestimenta prosternebant in via,    spread in the way their clothes and garments,
et clamabant dicentes:              and cried, saying:
Hosanna filio David:                Hosanna to the Son of David:
benedictus qui venit                Blessed is he that cometh
in nomine Domini.                   in the name of the Lord!
```
