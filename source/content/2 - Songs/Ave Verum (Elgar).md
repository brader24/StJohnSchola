# Ave Verum (Elgar)

## Lyrics
```
Ave verum corpus natum ex Maria Virgine,
vere passum, immolatum in cruce pro homine,
cujus latus perforatum vero fluxit et sanguine, esto nobis praegustatum mortis in examine.
O Clemens, O pie, O dulcis Jesu, Fili Mariae.

Hail, true body, born of the Virgin Mary,
who truly suffered, was sacrificed on the cross for mankind,
from whose pierced side flowed water and blood:
be for us a foretaste in the trial of death.
O sweet, O loving, O Jesus son of Mary.
```
