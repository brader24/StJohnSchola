# O Jesu Christe (Mantua)

## Lyrics
```
O Jesu Christe, miserere mei,
quia dolore langueo.
Domine, tu es spes mea.
Clamavi ad te, miserere mei.

O Jesus Christ, have mercy on me,
for I languish with sorrow.
Lord, you are my hope;
I have called to you, have mercy on me.
```
